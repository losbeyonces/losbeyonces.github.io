Los Beyoncés

Los Beyoncés are a young Duo from Germany and Switzerland which are quite new to the contemporary circus world. The two Women are involved in circus for a few years already, but were always behind the scenes. Now its time for them to appear in the lime light with their fresh, powerful and feminist performance „still genius, still idiots“. The Piece is their take on the famous Svalbard show, which sadly presents no female artists. The Duo gained fame within the German and Swiss circus community already, through their incredible Performance „Box office“, which they will also do this summer for the fifth time. If you want new faces, talented women and a whole lotta fun - book los Beyoncés!s
